About our business plans

Purchasing one of our business plans gives you what we call comparison points. These points can be redeemed for uploads of product comparison photos. Once you upload your comparisons to your business profile, they will immediately be live on Peanut.

Consumers will compare a business product everytime they upload one of their own comparisons, or if they hold an account, they can compare business products in return for micropayments to their accounts. You can view the analysis of any comparison at any time. Comparisons will stop once 100 users have provided opinions, if you require more feedback on certain products you can purchase additional opinions.

Your feedback will be given to you in an easy to understand format, and provide demographic insights such as gender bias. For custom packages please contact us directly.
